About

Welcome to Running8. Running8 aims to help match new runners with shoes appropriate for their gait.

Instructions

  1. Perform the wet test, by placing your wet foot on a sheet of paper
  2. Place the paper against a dark surface, and orient your footprint vertically
  3. Take a picture of the paper, with only the paper and footprint on screen. Alternatively you may crop the photo.
  4. Fill out the form, below and submit.
